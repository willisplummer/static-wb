All Feelings Of Alienation Are A
Shared Experience

Mira Gonzalez

That looming ‘off­ness’ I always felt with him
the constant knowledge that something was a little bit doomed
only intensified in the absence of his presence

The feeling sometimes became so immediate and insufferable
that I began to think my own life was something
I could no longer control with thoughts or actions

I maintained constant low­level paranoia
