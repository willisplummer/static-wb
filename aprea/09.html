Jonathan Aprea

Dyson's Aggregate

Dyson’s cortex, hands, his heart’s
motor, molecules, his face,
eyes, the 20 offices 3 children 39
salable products countless
patents hand dryers fans
heaters expanding in the
graying landscape Georgian
estate engineering building helipad
for helicopter french home large boat
townhouse small sports center
academic foundation his skin his hair
dust debris polycarbonate
acrylonitrile butadiene
styrene he wakes every day from
his bed sheets from the dark in a
wash he will fall back into.
