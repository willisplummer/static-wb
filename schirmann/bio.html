SIX CAIRNS FROM BOYFRIEND MOUNTAIN

Kelly Schirmann

KELLY SCHIRMANN is the author of Activity Book (NAP, 2014) & the co-author, with Tyler Brewington, of Boyfriend Mountain (forthcoming from Poor Claudia).

She sings in the band Young Family, whose EP King Cobra is available as a cassette from Spork Press, & runs Black Cake Records, a web-label for audio-chapbooks of contemporary poetry.

She lives in Portland, Oregon.
