poems

Ashley D'Arcy

the art of daria and the mellow beat that is in the recording

i can be really far out

i can get myself all hula hooping around

realizing that the frequency that the show daria is recorded on


is so

that if you play it really low

there it is:

very mellow


turn on daria

turn daria down way low

and you’ll find the mellow beat

that guides you along


while i’m peeing in my chair

i am lifted beyond my body because of the art
