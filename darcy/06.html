i don’t care if things are bad cus i’m good

i’m good

do you want to come be good with me

i’m good

do you want to come to bed with me

do you want to come be good to me

i’m in ecstasy

with the fruit growing from the trees

so, do you want to come to bed with me

i want you to talk to me

like how i’m talking to you

here

i want you to talk to me

i’m in ecstacy

come be good to me
