poems

Ashley D'Arcy

transcendent experience at dia: beacon

the window at dia: beacon tells me to BE

my new blouse is so delicate and beautiful

that when i walk thru the dia, i feel the forest

when i walk thru the dia, i feel the forest

the spirit moves through the ropes and the ropes move thru me

i’m calling out like a bird

i’m wandering thru the tops of trees
