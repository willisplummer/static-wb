Matthew Rohrer

POEM

They said my glasses
make me look fat
then the drunk kids headed home
to make everything
under the ballfield lights
pile up in the sky.
I can’t stand the heat
of the most beautiful day
and close my eyes.
I move within.
I follow a thought without socks
and the wind picks up,
it doesn’t seem real.
Are you playing guitar?
