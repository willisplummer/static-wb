LINGERIE SEASON

Niina Pollari

My bedroom is a lacquered piano that someone recently opened

They are taping together the piano wires right now to make space for everyone

Many historical things have been closed then opened for everyone

Time capsules, sarcophagi, bomb shelters

But those I mention are mostly men’s things

My bowels fill with emotionally upsetting warmth

At a mention of men’s things

Bowels are long body tubes that help

Carry things outside

I don’t know anyone in my room

But who can actually know anyone
